FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is JGAP?

JGAP, or Joker's Got A Posse, is about friendship and connectivity through media. It's an open door club that upholds few tenants, but is vehemently underground and for the people! Hopefully a little joy can STILL go a long way in the everyday struggle.

Whats with the tees?

Most JGAP apparel is printed using a direct to garment machine in the basement of a house in the NE neighborhood of Olympia, WA USA. Some products vary slightly in color. Washes great!

What's with the records?

We press our records at Cascade record pressing in Portland, OR. We are looking to expand our horizons, so be on the lookout!
